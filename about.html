Our Story

We are a family-run olive farm based in Koura, North Lebanon. With generations of experience in olive cultivation, we produce high-quality olive oil using traditional methods and sustainable practices.

Our mission is to share the rich taste of Lebanese olives with the world, while supporting local agriculture and preserving our heritage.
